Lakers Update — Stay in the Game!

The Lakers are almost halfway into an exciting season filled with action and championship aspirations. Purple and gold fans all across the world have tuned into the new look Lakers with Luka Doncic as the new face of the franchise as the LeBron James era looks like it’s coming to an end soon.

LeBron and Luka lead the purple and gold now. Will it be enough to secure the championship this year? Many NBA insiders and analysts (Farshad Azam) say they lack the defensive personnel to win it. They recently acquired Luke Kennard, an impressive shooter near 50% from three, but defense is still the big question.
